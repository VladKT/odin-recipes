Microwave Scrambled Eggs

This recipe is your basic scrambled eggs with just salt and pepper.

Ingredients:

  • cooking spray
  • 8 eggs
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Spray a large glass bowl with cooking spray. Crack 1 egg into a small ramekin and then pour into the bowl. Whisk egg. Repeat with remaining eggs, whisking well after each addition.
  2. Cook eggs in a microwave oven for 30 seconds; remove from oven and stir. Continue cooking in 30-second intervals, stirring after each, until eggs are almost set, about 3 minutes total. Season with salt and pepper.
